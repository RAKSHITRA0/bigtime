Company Wings

BigTime Company operates through independently managed business wings, each designed to dominate its respective domain while remaining strategically aligned with the parent conglomerate.

ACTIVE WING

BigTime Web & Tech Solutions

This wing handles digital infrastructure, software development, website creation, system deployment, PC assembly, and computer lab setup. It serves as the technological backbone of the conglomerate and its clients.

ACTIVE WING

BigTime Social

BigTime Social manages brand presence, digital visibility, content strategy, growth systems, and platform-level influence across major social media ecosystems.

ACTIVE WING

BigTime Brokerage

This wing facilitates high-value transactions by connecting serious buyers and sellers of large assets including real estate, commercial infrastructure, and strategic holdings.

ACTIVE WING

BigTime Strategic Networks

A controlled facilitation arm that leverages trusted networks to connect individuals and entities requiring access, influence, or specialized problem-solving capabilities.

Expansion Is Structural

Additional wings will be introduced as opportunities meet BigTime Company’s standards for control, scalability, and long-term relevance.
